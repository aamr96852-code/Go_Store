Apple Watch Series 11: The Future of Health. Now on Your Wrist.

The Apple Watch Series 11 is here, taking the world’s most advanced wearable to an entirely new level. It's the ultimate device for a healthy life, designed with groundbreaking features to provide you with deeper insights into your well-being, while still serving as the perfect workout partner and a vital connection to your world. This is our most powerful and personal Apple Watch yet—engineered to keep you informed, motivated, and safe.

Apple Watch 11. Smarter Insights. Stronger You.

The Series 11 provides a bigger picture of your health, but its biggest step is the pioneering Hypertension Notifications. This innovative feature can spot signs of chronic high blood pressure by analyzing how your blood vessels respond over 30 days—a crucial step in addressing a condition that often goes undiagnosed. Supported by advanced machine learning, the Apple Watch Series 11 also empowers you with the ECG app, a new Sleep Score to understand your rest quality, and the Vitals app to track daily metrics like heart rate and respiratory rate, ensuring you’re always informed.

Smarter Training Starts Here. Your Workout. Your Way.

Whether you're training for a marathon or just tracking your daily movement, Apple Watch Series 11 serves up endless ways to work out smarter. The refreshed Workout app makes accessing features like Pacer and Race Route quicker than ever, and with Workout Buddy, you get audible, personalized motivation in real time—it’s like having an Apple Fitness+ trainer right on your wrist. You can now easily monitor your Training Load to know when to push or recover and create completely Custom Workouts tailored to your exact pace and goals.

All-Day Battery Life. Stay Charged. Stay Ahead.

Longer battery life? You got it. The Apple Watch Series 11 delivers up to 24 hours of normal use, ensuring you can wear your watch all day and still track your critical overnight health data without worrying about a charge. Even with a huge leap in features, its powerful efficiency means you’ll enjoy the reliability you can count on. And if you’re running low, enjoy up to 8 hours of normal use with just a quick 15-minute fast charge—a huge benefit for active users in Egypt.

On the Go. A 5G Leap in Connectivity.

Experience new freedom with the Apple Watch Series 11. Now supporting speedy 5G cellular, you can download apps, music, and podcasts faster than ever, with better coverage even in areas with weak reception. With a cellular plan, you can confidently call, text, pay with Apple Pay, and stream music—all without your iPhone nearby. Find your misplaced iPhone using Precision Finding or use simple gestures like the wrist flick to manage calls and notifications—it’s all about convenience and staying connected.

A Real Lifeline. Always Watching Over You.

Packed with innovative safety features, the Apple Watch Series 11 is truly a guardian on your wrist. It's designed to summon help when you need it most, even if you can’t reach your phone. Features like Emergency SOS, Fall Detection, and Crash Detection can automatically connect you to emergency services, share your location, and notify loved ones. For daily peace of mind, use Check In to automatically notify someone when you arrive at your destination or let Backtrack guide you safely home when you’re off the grid.

The Smartest Watch. Intelligence at Hand.

The power of Apple Intelligence from your nearby iPhone now seamlessly enhances your Apple Watch experience. Get instant convenience with Smart Stack hints that deliver a gentle prompt when a suggestion is immediately useful, like reminding you it’s time for a jog. Live Translation now automatically translates texts in Messages, breaking down communication barriers right on your wrist. The Apple Watch Series 11 is a reflection of your daily routine—intuitive, proactive, and effortlessly helpful.
